About Me

Back in high school, I didn't have a clear vision for my life and having to choose a major after graduation put me in a bit of a dilemma😢. With no major in mind, I enrolled in core courses in an undergraduate economics curriculum in the belief that it will quickly land me a job😎. Introductory statistics was one of the greatest courses I took, it had an impact on me without even realizing it because I ended up buying a vintage statistics textbook from a local flea market and I went through that book thoroughly. One year later, I switched to statistics simply because I had a grasp of STEM topics. Luckily, I took pleasure in studying it and I had one great teacher to look up to, which later initiated my interest in data science.

When the outbreak of COVID-19 became a global pandemic😷, students were sent home and universities adopted digital remote learning policies (huge disappointment😞). However, every cloud has a silver lining because it finally led me to take drastic steps to learn more about the field of data science. I was intrigued by the combination of two disciplines that I have always wished I could major in both; computer science and statistics.

I developed my technical experience throughout internships and projects in different industries including banking, energy, and ride-hailing. I want to pursue my career in data science to undertake more projects and constantly learn and journey my way through data, a journey full of discovery, insights, and innovation.

Outside of academics and career pursuits, you'll probably find me running🏃 or playing soccer on the field🥅 with my friends under the night sky, aglow with bright city lights🌃.
